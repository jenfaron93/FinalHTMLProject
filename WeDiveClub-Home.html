We Dive Club was created in 2017 by Brian and Suzy Schultze when they realized there were no dive shops in the city of Saint Louis, Missouri. WDC gives divers the opportunity to purchase dive equipment, to take certified PADI dive classes, and to go on thrilling dive vacations where you can explore the ocean around the world.

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to explore a different part of our world? The ocean accounts for 70% of Earth - there is so much that is waiting to be explored. We have over 50 dive instructors ready to teach you what it’s like to be an open water diver. We offer classes like wreck diving, navigation, deep diving, and search & rescue diving. Once you become a PADI diver, you’re certified for life.
